# Privacy Policy - YouTube Speed Control

Last updated: January 2026

This Firefox extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

The extension stores only your preferred playback speed setting locally on your device using Firefox's built-in storage API (browser.storage.local). This data never leaves your device.

## No Data Collection

- No personal information collected
- No browsing history tracked
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cookies
- No external network requests
- No third-party services

## Permissions Explained

- **storage** - Required to save your speed preference locally
- **youtube.com** - Required to inject speed controls on YouTube pages
